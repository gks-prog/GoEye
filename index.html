01

Brand Vision

GOEYE should not be viewed as just another eyewear brand. My goal is for it to become an elevated lifestyle movement with genuine cultural impact — a name people integrate seamlessly into their daily lives, one that sets trends on major runways, is worn by top models, and carries prestige that extends well beyond the fashion industry. We are not simply selling glasses; we are building an iconic identity.

To scale this vision, our Instagram strategy needs a precise, high-energy balance between premium aesthetics and indirect, narrative-driven marketing. Rather than relying on aggressive, repetitive direct ads that dilute brand value, the focus should be on high-retention storytelling, elite collaborations, and compelling daily content that keeps audiences hooked. The objective is a page that naturally embeds GOEYE into the lifestyle of our audience — a brand they respect, remember, and engage with every day.

02

Instagram Audit Findings

What's Working

01

A Wide, Clearly Showcased Product Portfolio

GOEYE's Instagram effectively showcases a diverse range of eyewear, including prescription glasses, sunglasses, and sports and performance eyewear. This broad product mix positions the brand as a comprehensive eyewear destination rather than a niche seller, allowing it to appeal to multiple customer segments with different needs and lifestyles.

02

A Strong Myth-Busting Content Angle

Messaging such as “Carrots fix your vision. Dim light ruins your eyes. Glasses make you dependent — most of what you've heard is wrong” is confident and scroll-stopping. This debunking format is one of the highest-retention content strategies on Instagram: GOEYE isn't just selling glasses, it's rewiring what people think they already know.

03

A Clear, Ownable Bio Positioning

GOEYE's bio doesn't read like every other eyewear seller — it stakes a claim on being the brand that tells people “what nobody else tells you” about eye health and eyewear.

Areas of Improvement

Build a Strong Brand Community

The page currently functions primarily as a content and product showcase. To drive long-term engagement and customer loyalty, GOEYE should invest in recurring content series, customer spotlights, interactive Stories, polls, challenges, and a branded hashtag.

Optimize Profile & Content Fundamentals

The profile can be strengthened with a clearer value proposition and stronger call-to-action in the bio. Captions should carry a consistent brand voice and structured storytelling.

Shift from Direct Selling to Storytelling

The brand's premium positioning is a strong foundation, but Instagram rewards content that entertains before it sells.
